ACTS
OF
BEHIND THE MEMORIES


ACTS is a collection of short stories that explore the lives and experiences of the characters in "Behind the Memories". Each act delves into different aspects of their personalities, relationships, and the world they inhabit. Through these stories, readers can gain a deeper understanding of the characters and the themes that run throughout the main narrative.
