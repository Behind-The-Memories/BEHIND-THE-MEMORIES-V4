BEHIND THE MEMORIES

(BTM)


Behind The Memories is a phychological horror game that represents the developers life, also the game was made by 2 sixteen year old teens. The story beggins on August 2023 when a very important person for the developer died and 2 years later at September 2025 the developer started the idea for the game. This is why we are here now. If you look down you'll see some of the people that helped with the game, if you can, please support them.
